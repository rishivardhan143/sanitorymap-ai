Hygiene & Sanitation Best Practices

  • Wash hands frequently with soap and clean water.
  • Dispose of garbage in designated bins; avoid littering.
  • Keep toilets and drains clean; report blockages early.
  • Use safe drinking water and store it hygienically.
  • Prevent mosquito breeding: cover water containers and clear stagnant water.
